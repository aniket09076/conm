Unit 4: Approximations and ODEs

This section focuses on two important topics: Approximations and the Solution of Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs).

Key Concepts:

Approximation of Functions:

  • Taylor Series Representation: Represents a function as a sum of its derivatives, useful for finding approximate solutions.
  • Chebyshev Polynomials: Special polynomials that help minimize errors when approximating functions.
